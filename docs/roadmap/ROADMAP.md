# RepoSense Roadmap

## Phase 1: Backend Verifier OSS

- Harden Fact Engine and deterministic output contracts
- Stabilize backend transaction and side-effect signal visibility
- Keep CI/gate/report/manifest workflows reproducible

## Phase 2: AI Context Pack Standard

- Evolve context pack schemas and references for AI-assisted workflows
- Keep evidence traceability and deterministic compatibility

## Phase 3: Maintain Mode

- Add change-readiness and upgrade-focused inspection workflows
- Improve long-lived service maintainability support

## Phase 4: AI Insight Cloud

- Provide managed insight orchestration over OSS outputs
- Add richer guided explain/prioritize flows with explicit grounding boundaries

## Phase 5: Team / Enterprise Reporting

- Team-level risk tracking and governance views
- Enterprise reporting templates and policy integrations
